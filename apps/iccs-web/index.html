How It Works

Our Proven Recovery Process

A transparent, regulatory-compliant methodology designed for GCC banking environments.

01

Portfolio Onboarding

Secure data transfer of your delinquent accounts. We perform full KYC validation, account segmentation, and prioritization within 48 hours.

02

Debtor Contact Strategy

Multi-channel outreach through calls, SMS, WhatsApp, email, and field visits — fully compliant with GCC consumer protection regulations.
